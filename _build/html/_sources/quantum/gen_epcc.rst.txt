
gen_epcc: Electron-phonon coupled cluster equations
---------------------------------------------------

**Package:** https://github.com/awhite862/gen_epcc

This repository contains automatically generated equations for the theories presented in this work:

**Reference:** White, Alec F., Yang Gao, Austin J. Minnich, and Garnet Kin-Lic Chan. "A coupled cluster framework for electrons and phonons." *The Journal of Chemical Physics* **153**, (2020): 224112. doi:`10.1063/5.0033132 <https://doi.org/10.1063/5.0033132>`_
