
fted: Finite Temperature Exact Diagonalization
==============================================

**Package:** https://github.com/sunchong137/fted

Using exact diagonalization to solve the finite temperature problems of both model and molecular systems.

Based on grand canonical ensemble.

**Author:** Chong Sun

**Reference:** Sun, C., Ray, U., Cui, Z. H., Stoudenmire, M., Ferrero, M., & Chan, G. K. L. (2020). Finite-temperature density matrix embedding theory. *Physical Review B*, **101**, 075131. doi:`10.1103/PhysRevB.101.075131 <https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevB.101.075131>`_
